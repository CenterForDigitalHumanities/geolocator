Welcome to the RERUM Geolocator hosted by the Research Computing Group at Saint Louis University. Here you can enhance Linked Open Data resource by providing geospatial descriptors. This does not affect the original resource nor does it notify the resource owner of the enhancement. There is no user system and so data generated here cannot be attributed to you specifically. However, it will be available to you and other applications to make it your own.

This application is intended to work within the scope of the IIIF Presentation API and encourages you to bring your own IIIF resources. If your web resource fits this category, there are two main paths to take.
